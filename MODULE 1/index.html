Community News

Upcoming Community Festival

Join us for the annual community festival featuring local artists, musicians, and food vendors. The event will take place in Central Park from June 15-17. Admission is free for all residents.

We're excited to announce that this year's festival will include a special children's area with interactive games and educational activities. Local schools will be participating with student performances throughout the weekend.

New Community Center Opening

The long-awaited community center is finally opening its doors next month. The facility includes a state-of-the-art gymnasium, meeting rooms, and a public library extension.

Membership registration will begin next week, with special early-bird rates for the first 100 members. The center will offer various programs for all age groups, from toddlers to seniors.
